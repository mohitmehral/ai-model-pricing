What are Tokens?
When you run inference with AI models, you're charged for the number of tokens in your input and output. Tokens are text pieces that models process - different providers count tokens differently. What is Google Vertex AI pricing for Anthropic Claude and Cohere models?

Google Cloud Vertex AI offers Anthropic Claude models (Claude 3.5 Sonnet at $3/$15 per million tokens, Claude 3 Haiku at $0.25/$1.25) and Cohere models (Command R+ at $2.50/$10, Command R at $0.50/$1.50). Use the GCP filter above to compare all Vertex AI models including Google Gemini, Anthropic Claude, and Cohere Command. Pricing is sourced from official Google Cloud documentation.

How much does Anthropic Claude cost on Google Vertex AI?

Anthropic Claude pricing on Google Cloud Vertex AI varies by model: Claude 3.5 Sonnet costs $3.00 per million input tokens and $15.00 per million output tokens, while Claude 3 Haiku is more economical at $0.25/$1.25 per million tokens. These prices are sourced from official Google Cloud documentation and may vary by region. Always confirm the latest pricing with Google Cloud Vertex AI before deploying production workloads.

How does Anthropic Claude pricing on Vertex AI compare to Cohere and OpenAI?

On Google Vertex AI, Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet ($3/$15 per million tokens) is competitively priced against OpenAI GPT-4 Turbo ($10/$30) but more expensive than Cohere Command R+ ($2.50/$10). For cost-sensitive workloads, Cohere Command R ($0.50/$1.50) and Claude 3 Haiku ($0.25/$1.25) offer budget-friendly alternatives.
